12 Months of...

Tools: 4k Video Downloader, Adobe Premiere Pro

In the assignment, we are supposed to create a 2 minute video of 10 second clips about 12 months of something. So I chose to create the first 12 months of a puppies life! I first used a program called 4k Video Downloader to get clips of puppies in different stages of the first 12 months of their lives on the internet. I then utiliized Premiere Pro to put the clips together and created the video with met criteria.

Video of the first 12 months of a puppy's life!
