New Species

Tool: Adobe Photoshop

When first creating this special little monster of mine, I wanted to get an animal that is so puny, yet it thinks it's the king. After a bit of brainstorming, I landed on pigeon. Now, what is an animal that is actually connsidered the king of something? A lion! So then I used a lion head to cover the pigeon head to show how the pigeon thinks. Lastly, since I needed at least one more animal included in this project, I decided to get a super puny animal. A fish. Not only did I find this funny to do, I think it kind of shows the duality of a pigeon, thinks it's mighty, yet it is so puny. Reminds me of anotherrr animal, a Chihuahua.
