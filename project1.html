Glitch Art

Tools: Text Edits, Adobe Photoshop

I first found the original image from the internet, which was a picture of MF DOOM's mask and one of Nujabes's album covers. I first used text edits to give it that glitched look, then I took the image to Photoshop and then copied some small parts of the picture and duplicated it all over the picture like pillars. Lastly, I scaled the image down and duplicated it on the top and bottom so it could get that old btoken tv look to it.

Glitch Art made from Nujabes x MF DOOM
